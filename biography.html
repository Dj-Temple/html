About DJ Temple

My name is LAWRENCE TOSIN VICENT [DJ TEMPLE], a passionate and dedicated Disc Jockey with a love for music that started at a young age. I am currently [25] years old, and over the years, I have worked tirelessly to hone my craft and bring the joy of music to people from all walks of life.

This fueled my dream to create unforgettable musical experiences that would resonate with audiences long after the music stopped. From childhood, I immersed myself in music, learning to mix beats, curate playlists, and read the energy of a crowd.

Now, at [25], I have built a reputation as one of the most engaging DJs in the industry. My ability to seamlessly blend genres like Afrobeat, hip-hop, EDM, reggae, and more has allowed me to perform at various events, including weddings, clubs, corporate gatherings, and festivals. With every performance, I strive to connect with the audience, creating memories that last a lifetime.

Beyond being a performer, I see myself as an ambassador of joy and connection through music. My mission is to entertain and inspire, whether it’s by setting the perfect mood for an event or helping upcoming DJs find their voice in the industry.

When I’m not performing, I enjoy exploring new music trends, collaborating with other artists, and sharing my journey with fans on social media. Music is not just my career; it’s my life, my passion, and my way of bringing people together.
